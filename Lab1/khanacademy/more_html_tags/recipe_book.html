Instant Ramen

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Instant ramen 1 pc
Water 500 ml

Step 1: Boil the water.

Step 2: Put the noodles in the water for 3 mins.

Step 3: Pour out the water.

Step 4: Pour the red packet of seasoning.

Step 5: Stir, stir, stir!
